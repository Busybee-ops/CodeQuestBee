My Journey

My fascination with computers began the day I first saw one at a bank. I was in school when my father, a bank officer, introduced me to it, explaining how the system stored vast amounts of data, processed transactions, and maintained financial records with precision. I was wide-eyed with curiosity, realizing that behind every seamless operation was a structured system of logic, data flow, and computation. That moment changed everything for me—I knew I wanted to work with computers, not just to use them, but to understand how they think, process information, and interact with the world.

From that day forward, I was drawn into the mechanics of computing—how bits and bytes translated into instructions, how data structures formed the foundation of complex applications, and how algorithms acted as the driving force behind decision-making. My journey into software development has been fueled by the desire to not only build applications but to truly comprehend the underlying principles that make computers powerful tools for problem-solving.

My Projects
  • Water Intake Calculator

    A sophisticated hydration tool that dynamically calculates optimal daily water consumption based on weather conditions and body weight. Using arithmetic logic combined with an AI-driven model, the system analyzes temperature, humidity, and personal weight to adapt hydration recommendations. This ensures a balance between scientific precision and practical usability, making the tool responsive to environmental and physiological changes.

  • Weather Information App

    A real-time weather analysis system that connects to a weather API and transforms raw data into meaningful insights. Beyond simply displaying forecasts, the application structures and visualizes information in multiple formats—graphs, interactive charts, and summarized reports—allowing users to comprehend patterns and trends with clarity. This project showcases my ability to work with APIs, handle large datasets, and implement effective data visualization techniques.

  • Tic-Tac-Toe AI Game

    A strategic game where the AI opponent is nearly unbeatable—but not entirely. Built using minimax algorithm and reinforcement learning, the AI evaluates every possible move, predicts the player’s strategy, and optimizes its gameplay accordingly. However, with precise moves, the player can find a guaranteed winning path, making the game not only challenging but also an intellectual puzzle where one must "out-think" the computer. This project highlights my deep understanding of game theory, decision trees, and algorithmic strategy.

For me, computers are more than just machines—they are structured systems built on logic, data, and patterns. Understanding them is like deciphering a language of possibilities, where every algorithm, every data point, and every interaction has meaning. I am constantly exploring new technologies, refining my knowledge, and developing solutions that not only function efficiently but also demonstrate the depth of computational intelligence.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and check out my projects. Let’s build something amazing together!
